Thimbles

The idea of the game is as simple as possible: three thimbles and only one ball. The ball is placed under one cap and they move fast enough and you have to guess where the ball ended up. If you played thimbles at the station, then the ball would not be in a glass, but in the hands of a lucky cheat, but if with a phone or tablet, then everything is without deceit.
