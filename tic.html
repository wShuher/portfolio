Tic Tac Toe

Everyone knows the good old tic-tac-toe. Yes, they are fun to play... If you are 5 years old. But then comes the understanding that whoever goes first wins. Well, or, if the opponent is also not 5 years old, it all comes down to a draw.
In this game, it's not that easy. Here you have to play on a volumetric cube, which has not one field, but six! Almost 3D. After each move, the face of the cube moves in a random direction, and you have a different picture in front of you. Well, of course, the goal of the game is to put three crosses in succession and prevent the enemy from doing the same with zeros.
